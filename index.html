About me

As a 21-year-old from the amazing city of Fresno, California, I have always been captivated by the power of cameras to freeze moments in time and tell stories. My family, consisting of four energetic brothers, has been a constant source of love and support, making my childhood a truly wonderful one. During my time in Chile, I had the privilege of serving as a missionary for my church, an experience that taught me invaluable life lessons and allowed me to immerse myself in a new culture for two years.
